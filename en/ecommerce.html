Sell online without formalities — FDK incubator for e-commerce

Why is the FDK incubator ideal for e-commerce?

Online sales is a dynamically growing industry where speed and low operating costs matter. The FDK incubator allows you to run an online store, sell on platforms like Allegro, Amazon, Etsy, or your own e-shop — without registering your own company. You use our legal personality, NIP, and full accounting support.

Thanks to the incubator, you can significantly reduce your business operating costs. This is a huge advantage over a traditional sole proprietorship, where formal costs can consume a significant portion of your margin.

Note: No obligation to pay ZUS contributions applies only to students under 26 years of age.

FDK also supports sellers working with payment operators such as PayU, Przelewy24, or Stripe. We ensure proper invoicing, tax settlements, and ongoing advisory.
