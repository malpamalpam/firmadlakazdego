About us

Firma Dla Każdego Foundation

We have been with you since 2015, helping you realize your dreams of running a business. We are the highest-rated business incubator in Poland.

We serve clients from around the world in Polish, English, Ukrainian, and Russian. Our team consists of experienced specialists in accounting, law, HR, and foreigner services.

We offer comprehensive support — from business idea pre-incubation, through full bookkeeping and invoicing, to legalization of residence and work of foreigners in Poland.
