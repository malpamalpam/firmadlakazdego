Code and design without formalities — FDK incubator for IT

Why is the FDK incubator ideal for programmers and designers?

As a freelance programmer or designer, your value lies in your technical and creative skills — not in filling out ZUS declarations or settling income tax. The FDK incubator takes over all formal, accounting, and legal support, so you can focus solely on coding, designing, and completing projects. You issue B2B invoices, cooperate with domestic and foreign clients, and we take care of the rest.

Within the incubator, you do not need to register your own business (sole proprietorship). You use the Foundation's legal personality, which means no ZUS contributions (saving over PLN 1,600 per month!) and a preferential income tax of just 6%. These are real savings that allow you to invest more in equipment, training, or simply keep more of your earnings.

FDK works excellently among IT freelancers cooperating with platforms such as Upwork, Toptal, or Deel. Whether you work for Polish companies or foreign clients — the incubator provides full invoicing, settlement, and advisory support. Join thousands of satisfied programmers and designers who already cooperate with us.
