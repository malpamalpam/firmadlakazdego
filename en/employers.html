For Employers

Optimize employment costs through B2B cooperation

Do you employ workers? Looking for a cost-effective and secure way to settle with them via invoices?

B2B cooperation with a business incubator will optimize your employment costs. More and more companies are choosing the B2B model — the employer significantly reduces employment costs, while the worker gains a better negotiating position and higher net salary.

The FDK incubator takes over the formalities related to settling your associates. Instead of many employment or civil-law contracts — you receive one VAT invoice for all employees cooperating through the incubator. This solution is particularly attractive for companies in IT, marketing, translation, design, and other intellectual services.

How does it work?

3 simple steps to optimize employment costs

Traditional B2B cooperation requires the worker to register their own company (sole proprietorship). This involves paying ZUS (over PLN 1,600/month), full bookkeeping, and tax settlements. Through the FDK incubator, the worker avoids these costs and formalities while offering you the same quality of B2B cooperation.

Through B2B cooperation via the incubator, you eliminate employer costs (ZUS, OHS training, medical examinations, holidays). You receive one VAT invoice that you can deduct from taxes. The worker in turn receives a higher net salary thanks to a lower PIT (6% instead of the standard 12-32%).

A worker cooperating through the incubator gains: independence in insurance decisions, flexible working conditions, the ability to deduct income-generating costs, no ZUS (for work-for-hire contracts), low income tax (6%), and full accounting and legal support within the subscription.
