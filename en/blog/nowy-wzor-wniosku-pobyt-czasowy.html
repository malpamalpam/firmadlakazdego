New Temporary Residence Application Form Since December 2025 — What Changed?

FDK Team · 05.01.2026

Table of Contents

Legal basis for changes

The new template for the temporary residence permit application was introduced by a regulation of the Minister of Internal Affairs and Administration dated November 28, 2025. The regulation came into force on December 15, 2025, meaning that from this date all applications must be submitted on the new form. Applications submitted on the old form after this date will be returned with a request for resubmission.

The form change is part of a broader reform of the foreigner service system in Poland, related to the implementation of the MOS system and adaptation to new EU directives. The new form was designed for electronic data processing, which is expected to speed up case handling.

What changed in the form?

The most important change is the simplification of the application structure. The previous form was 8 pages and contained many repetitive fields. The new form has been shortened to 6 pages, and duplicated fields have been eliminated. At the same time, new sections related to digital identification of the applicant have been added.

The graphic layout of the form has changed — the new template is clearer, with clearly separated sections and filling instructions placed directly next to the relevant fields. Mandatory fields are marked with an asterisk, making it easier to identify information that must be filled in.

New fields and sections

The new form includes a section for electronic correspondence address. The foreigner provides an email address to which notifications about changes in case status will be sent. This is a mandatory field and replaces the previous mail-only communication, significantly speeding up correspondence with the office.

A mobile phone number field with country code has also been added, enabling SMS contact in urgent matters. A new "Additional information" section allows the applicant to describe special circumstances that may affect the case — e.g., pregnancy, illness, documented family ties in Poland.

How to correctly fill out the new application

The application should be filled in with block letters, using a black or blue pen. For electronic completion (fillable PDF form), use a font size of at least 10 points. All text fields are filled in Polish, except for first and last names, which are given according to the travel document.

The section regarding the purpose of stay requires special attention. Mark the appropriate purpose (work, studies, family reunification, business activity, etc.) and provide detailed justification in the description field. Generic phrases like "I want to work in Poland" may result in a request for supplementation, extending the proceedings.

Most common mistakes

The most common mistake is using the old application form. Since December 15, 2025, voivodeship offices accept only applications on the new form. Before submitting, check that the form has the current regulation date in the footer. Old forms are unfortunately still available on many websites.

Another common mistake is incomplete filling of the section on previous stays in the Schengen area. The new form requires detailed information about all stays in the last 5 years, including entry and exit dates, basis of stay, and country of stay.

Where to download the new form

The official new application form is available on the Office for Foreigners website (udsc.gov.pl) under the "Application forms" tab. The form can be downloaded in PDF format for printing or in an interactive version for computer completion. We recommend using official sources only to avoid the risk of downloading an outdated or modified form.

The form is also available in the MOS system after logging into the user account. When submitting an application electronically through MOS, the form is generated automatically based on data entered into the system — there is no need to download or fill it in separately.
