Sports and Medical Packages

MultiSport, Medicover, and healthcare for foreigners in Poland

MultiSport Card

The MultiSport card is the most popular sports benefit in Poland, providing access to over 4,500 sports and recreational facilities across the country. Through cooperation with the FDK incubator, foreigners working in Poland can use the MultiSport card on the same terms as full-time employees of large corporations.

MultiSport enables use of gyms, swimming pools, fitness clubs, group classes, squash, tennis, climbing, and many other activities -- with unlimited entries. The card is active from the first day of the month and works throughout Poland, which is especially important for people who frequently travel between cities.

We offer MultiSport cards in Classic, Plus, and Kids variants -- both for employees themselves and for their families. Card activation is quick and does not require additional formalities beyond registration through the incubator.

Medicover Medical Packages

Medicover medical packages provide comprehensive healthcare -- from specialist consultations to laboratory and imaging diagnostics. Foreigners working in Poland through the FDK incubator can benefit from private healthcare without the long waits typical of the public health system.

The medical packages include access to internal medicine, pediatric, gynecological, ophthalmological, dermatological, and many other specialist consultations. Packages also cover laboratory tests, ultrasound, X-ray, ECG, and teleconsultations -- convenient for those who prefer remote medical appointments.

Various package options are available -- from basic to extended -- tailored to individual health needs. Packages can also cover family members of the employee residing in Poland.

Who Is It For?

Sports and medical packages are available to all foreigners cooperating with the Firma Dla Każdego incubator under B2B contracts. Nationality, visa type, and length of cooperation do not matter -- the benefit is available from the first month.

Programmers, graphic designers, translators, e-commerce specialists, architects, musicians, and representatives of other industries who settle accounts through the incubator can all take advantage of the offer. The packages are especially popular among people who have recently moved to Poland and want to quickly take care of their health and physical activity.

Employees' families -- spouses, partners, and children -- can also be covered by sports and medical packages on preferential terms. This is an important element of support for foreigners building their lives in Poland.

How to Order?

Ordering a sports or medical package is simple and quick. Simply contact us by phone, email, or through the contact form on the website, and our team will present the available options and help you choose the best one.

After selecting a package, activation takes place from the first day of the following month. The MultiSport card is delivered in plastic or digital form (via mobile app), and the medical package is activated in the Medicover system -- appointment registration is possible immediately after activation.

The cost of packages is added to the monthly incubator subscription and settled on a single invoice. There are no hidden fees or long-term commitments -- you can change or cancel the package with one month's notice.
