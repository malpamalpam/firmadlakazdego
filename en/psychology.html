How does the FDK incubator work for psychologists?

Online psychology is growing rapidly — video consultations, psychoeducational webinars, developmental workshops and corporate wellbeing programs are increasingly popular forms of work. Many psychologists start private practice alongside employment, and a sole proprietorship with ZUS over PLN 1,825 per month is too costly to begin with.

The FDK incubator lets you provide online psychological consultations on B2B terms without your own company. You join the Foundation and operate under its legal personality. FDK issues invoices to your clients — companies ordering wellbeing workshops, psychology platforms, organizations needing psychological support. You receive your fee minus 6% PIT and the PLN 400 subscription.

Original therapeutic programs, psychoeducational materials, diagnostic tools and workbooks may qualify as copyrighted works. This makes it possible to apply 50% tax-deductible costs (KUP), giving an effective tax rate of just 6%.

Important: FDK handles online psychological counseling, developmental consultations, psychoeducation, psychological coaching and wellbeing trainings. We do not handle clinical practice requiring registration as a healthcare entity.

Why is FDK a good solution for psychologists?

Original programs and materials — 50% tax-deductible costs

A psychologist who creates their own therapeutic programs, psychoeducational materials, diagnostic questionnaires, workbooks or online courses creates copyrighted works. Original publications in the field of psychology — with their own methodology, exercises and structure — qualify as works, giving the right to 50% tax-deductible costs and an effective 6% tax rate.

Online counseling vs. clinical practice

FDK handles online psychological counseling — developmental consultations, psychoeducation, psychological coaching, crisis support. These are advisory and educational services that do not require registration as a healthcare entity. If you provide online consultations of a developmental and psychoeducational nature — FDK is the ideal solution. Clinical practice (psychotherapy within healthcare entities) is subject to separate regulations.

Wellbeing workshops and EAP programs for companies

Companies are increasingly investing in employee mental health. Stress management workshops, resilience programs, communication trainings, mindfulness sessions — all are B2B services that companies pay for with invoices. FDK issues VAT invoices to corporations and handles framework agreements with HR departments.

International clients — psychology without borders

Online psychology knows no geographical borders. Many Polish psychologists provide consultations for the Polish diaspora abroad or English-speaking clients. FDK handles invoices in foreign currencies and tax procedures for EU and non-EU clients. You can build an international practice without your own sole proprietorship.
