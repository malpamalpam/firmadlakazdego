Students under 26 do not pay ZUS or income tax regardless of the venture. Persons working under a work-for-hire (dzieło) contract are exempt from ZUS, as payment is based on a work-for-hire agreement. In trade industries (e-commerce, dropshipping), settlement is via a mandate contract, generating ZUS and income tax (44%) obligations for persons without student status under 26 or another insurance title.

You pay VAT (unless you benefit from a subject-matter exemption), income tax (6% or 9.6% depending on the activity), and possibly ZUS in trade industries. Persons under 26 do not pay income tax.
