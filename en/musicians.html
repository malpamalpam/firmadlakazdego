Create music without bureaucracy — FDK incubator for artists

Why is the FDK incubator ideal for musicians?

Music is a passion that increasingly becomes a source of income. Whether you play concerts, give instrument lessons, record in a studio, or create musical content online — you need a legal framework for settling your income. The FDK incubator allows you to run your artistic activity without registering your own company, without ZUS contributions, and with a minimum income tax of 6%.

For musicians whose income can be irregular and seasonal, the lack of mandatory ZUS contributions (over PLN 1,600 per month) is a huge financial relief. In the FDK incubator, you only pay the monthly subscription and tax on actual revenue. In months when you have fewer orders, your costs are proportionally lower.

FDK serves musicians cooperating with clubs, event agencies, music schools, and streaming platforms. We issue invoices, handle bookkeeping, and provide legal advisory. Join the group of artists who settle smartly and save on formalities.
