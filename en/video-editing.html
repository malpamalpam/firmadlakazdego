How does the FDK incubator work for video editors?

Video editing is project-based work — today you edit a commercial, tomorrow a YouTube vlog, next week a corporate training film. Each project means a different client, a different budget and a different deadline. Registering a sole proprietorship with fixed ZUS contributions over PLN 1,825 per month is unprofitable, especially when assignments are seasonal or irregular.

The FDK incubator lets you work on B2B terms without your own company. You join the Foundation and use its legal personality. FDK issues invoices to your clients — advertising agencies, production houses, YouTubers, companies needing video content. You receive your fee minus only 6% PIT and the PLN 400 subscription. Zero ZUS, zero accounting, zero CEIDG.

Video editing is creative audiovisual work — the choice of shots, editing rhythm, color palette, special effects and sound create a unique piece. This allows the editor to benefit from 50% tax-deductible costs (KUP), meaning the tax base is only half of the fee. Combined with the 12% tax scale, this gives an effective tax burden of just 6%.

Why is FDK the ideal solution for video editors?

Video editing as a creative work — 50% tax-deductible costs

Film editing is recognized as creative audiovisual work. The editor makes dozens of creative decisions: shot selection, narrative pace, transitions, color grading, sound — all of this constitutes a unique piece. Under copyright law, audiovisual material is a work, and its creator has the right to 50% tax-deductible costs. Combined with the 12% tax rate, this gives an effective PIT of 6%.

Working with agencies and production houses

Production houses, advertising agencies and post-production studios expect VAT invoices from their subcontractors. FDK handles all of these requirements — issues invoices, prepares framework agreements and ensures timely documentation. You do not need your own company to professionally serve corporate clients.

Editing for YouTubers and online creators

The editing market for online creators is growing rapidly. YouTubers, TikTokers, podcasters — all need dedicated editors. FDK allows you to invoice these clients on B2B terms, even if they are individuals running a business. We handle both one-off assignments and ongoing monthly collaboration.

Motion design and visual effects

Motion design, animations, VFX and compositing are highly creative forms of work that clearly qualify as copyrighted works. If you also create animations, intros/outros, lower thirds or animated infographics alongside editing — all of this can be settled with 50% KUP through FDK.

International clients

Video editing is fully remote work — your clients can be anywhere in the world. FDK handles invoices in foreign currencies, applies the reverse charge procedure for EU clients and advises on settling payments from freelancer platforms. You can work for clients from the US, Germany, UK or Scandinavia without your own sole proprietorship.
