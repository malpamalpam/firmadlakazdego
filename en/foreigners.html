Foreigners

A wide range of services for foreigners who want to legally work and reside in Poland

Firma Dla Każdego Foundation is the highest-rated business incubator in Poland, offering comprehensive services for foreigners. We enable legal work and residence in Poland through B2B cooperation with clients and VAT invoice issuance — without the need to register your own company.

We serve clients in Polish, English, Ukrainian, and Russian. Our team of legalization specialists will guide you through the entire process — from obtaining a visa to a residence permit.

Our services for foreigners

Comprehensive support in 4 languages

Residence and work legalization

Work visas, temporary and permanent residence permits, work permits, EU Blue Card, Polish Card, Polish citizenship. We handle cases at first and second instance, file appeals and complaints to administrative courts.
